# Answer

Donald Trump is an American businessman, television personality, and politician. He was the 45th President of the United States from 2017 to 2021.

Here are some key points about Donald Trump:

* Born on June 14, 1946, in Queens, New York
* Graduated from the University of Pennsylvania with a degree in economics
* Built his business career as a real estate developer and investor
* Hosted the reality TV show "The Apprentice" from 2004 to 2015
* Announced his candidacy for President in 2015 and won the Republican nomination in 2016
* Elected as the 45th President of the United States in 2016, defeating Democratic nominee Hillary Clinton
